History

Welcome to Recordium, where the rhythm of life meets the timeless charm of vinyl! We are a passion-driven indie vinyl record business founded by three friends who share a deep love for music and a commitment to preserving the soulful experience of vinyl. At Recordium, we believe in the magic that happens when the needle meets the groove, creating an authentic connection between the artist and the listener. Our journey began with a shared dream to revive the art of collecting and playing vinyl records, bringing back the warmth, crackle, and nostalgia that only vinyl can deliver.
